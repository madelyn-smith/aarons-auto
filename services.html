Services

Undercoating

A vehicle is often one of the largest purchases of your lifetime. Unfortunately, salted roads during the harsh NY winters can destroy your investment and cause costly repairs.

Undercoating is a cost-effective, practical solution that improves the life and resale value of your vehicle!

Our undercoating service fee includes 15 minutes of vehicle prep time. We use NHOU® (NH Oil Undercoating) Mouse-Out on your vehicle’s body and frame cavities and apply FLUID FILM® to the undercarriage.

Detailing

We offer several detailing packages based on your needs. Exterior wash, interior clean, wheels and tires, and glass cleaning services are available.

We can also perform paint decontamination and correction. Please contact us for a custom quote.

Auto Body Repair

Our team is experienced and equipped to repair auto body damage from even the toughest collisions. We only use original equipment (OEM), never aftermarket parts.

Our available services include body and fender repair, professional refinishing, paint touch-ups, and more. We also perform free estimates for your convenience. View our rates.
